✨ About StudyForge

StudyForge v5 is a free AI-powered study platform. Supports PDF, image, PowerPoint, and text input. 14 study modes including spaced-repetition flashcards, timed exam mode, essay marking, mind maps, glossary, mnemonics, translation, and simplification. Includes Pomodoro timer, timetable + exam planner, study plan generator, full analytics with charts, leaderboard, challenge-a-friend, multiplayer study room with whiteboard, difficulty levels, keyboard shortcuts, sound effects, and print-to-PDF. No login required.
